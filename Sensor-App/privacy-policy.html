Privacy Policy

Shreyash Pattewar built SensorApp as a free app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

SensorApp does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers to any external servers, third parties, or the developer.

Sensor Data

  • All sensor readings are processed locally on your device.
  • Logged sensor data is stored only in the app's local Room database on your device.
  • No sensor data is ever sent over the network or shared with any third party.
  • You can export sensor data as a CSV file to your Downloads folder — this stays on your device unless you manually share it.

Permissions

ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION and BODY_SENSORS are used only when you tap the relevant sensor. Data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

SensorApp does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally. Delete it via Settings, App Info → Clear data, or uninstall the app.
